# Requirements Document: Demo Assets

## Introduction

Demo assets for the LMA EDGE Hackathon presentation including video script, business metrics, pitch deck outline, and sample data.

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Video Script

**User Story:** As a presenter, I want a 90-second demo video script.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE video script SHALL be 90 seconds total
2. THE script SHALL cover: Problem (0:00-0:20), Single Demo (0:20-0:45), Batch (0:45-1:10), Impact (1:10-1:30)
3. THE script SHALL include on-screen text for key metrics

### Requirement 2: Business Metrics

**User Story:** As a presenter, I want quantified business metrics.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE metrics SHALL include time efficiency (90% reduction)
2. THE metrics SHALL include cost savings ($2M/year per 100 officers)
3. THE metrics SHALL include market opportunity ($5T TAM)

### Requirement 3: Pitch Deck

**User Story:** As a presenter, I want a pitch deck outline.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE pitch deck SHALL be 5 minutes presentation
2. THE pitch deck SHALL cover: Problem, Solution, Demo, Impact, Market, Ask

### Requirement 4: Sample Data

**User Story:** As a developer, I want sample borrower data for demos.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE sample data SHALL include 5 demo borrowers
2. THE sample data SHALL cover all decision types (APPROVED, REVIEW, REJECTED)
3. THE sample data SHALL include expected scores for consistency
